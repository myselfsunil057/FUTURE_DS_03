## 📊 Business Questions

This dashboard is designed to answer core business performance, customer behavior, and conversion optimization questions across the full e-commerce funnel.

---

### 🧭 Funnel Performance & Conversion
- What percentage of users move from product views → cart → purchase?
- Where is the **largest drop-off point** in the customer journey?
- How efficient is the platform in converting traffic into revenue?
- What is the overall conversion rate and how does it vary over time?

---

### 👁️ User Behavior & Engagement
- How do users interact with products before making a purchase decision?
- Which categories generate the highest attention (views) but lowest engagement?
- What behavioral patterns indicate purchase intent vs casual browsing?
- How significant is the **attention loss rate** in overall performance?

---

### 🛒 Cart & Checkout Analysis
- What percentage of users abandon their cart before completing purchase?
- Which product categories have the highest cart abandonment rates?
- What is the monetary value of abandoned carts?
- What friction points could be causing checkout drop-offs?

---

### 📦 Category & Product Performance
- Which product categories contribute most to revenue and orders?
- Which categories have the highest conversion efficiency (view → order)?
- Are there underperforming but high-potential categories?
- Is revenue concentrated in a few categories (risk analysis)?

---

### 📈 Revenue & Growth Trends
- How is revenue trending over time (MoM growth patterns)?
- What drove the **+120% revenue spike in Week 46**?
- Is growth driven by consistent performance or event-based spikes?
- What is the average revenue per order and how stable is it?

---

### 🚀 Strategic Growth Opportunities
- Which part of the funnel offers the highest ROI improvement potential?
- Where should marketing investment be prioritized (traffic, cart, or conversion)?
- Which categories should be scaled for maximum revenue impact?
- What strategies can replicate high-performing periods like Week 46?
