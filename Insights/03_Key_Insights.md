## 🔑 Key Insights

This dashboard reveals critical behavioral, revenue, and funnel efficiency patterns across a high-scale e-commerce platform processing 100M+ user interactions.

---

### 🚨 1. Severe Top-of-Funnel Leakage
- Only **3.8% of users** move from views → cart
- A massive **96.2% attention loss rate** indicates weak product-page engagement
- Primary bottleneck is **user interest conversion, not checkout**

👉 Interpretation: The platform is failing at “first impression → intent” conversion.

---

### 🛒 2. Moderate Checkout Friction but High Intent Drop
- **58% cart abandonment rate**
- Indicates strong intent users are still not completing purchases
- Likely causes: pricing surprises, trust gaps, or checkout complexity

👉 Interpretation: Even motivated users are being lost late in the funnel.

---

### 💰 3. Strong Monetization Efficiency
- Revenue per order: **$304 (high AOV)**
- Indicates strong product mix (Electronics-heavy basket)
- Monetization is strong when conversion happens

👉 Interpretation: The issue is volume, not value.

---

### 📈 4. Growth is Event-Driven, Not Organic
- **+120% MoM revenue growth**
- Driven by **Week 46 spike across all metrics**
- No evidence of consistent baseline scaling

👉 Interpretation: Growth depends on campaigns, not stable user behavior.

---

### 📦 5. Category Imbalance Creates Structural Risk
- Electronics dominates revenue and traffic
- “Kids” category shows **highest conversion (~50%) but low traffic**
- “Unknown” category distorts analytics reliability

👉 Interpretation: Portfolio is efficient but not optimized.

---

### ⚡ 6. Hidden High-Performance Segment Exists
- Kids, Accessories, Furniture show strong conversion efficiency
- These categories are underutilized in traffic allocation

👉 Interpretation: Growth potential is hidden in low-visibility, high-conversion segments.

---

### 🚀 8. Scaling Opportunity is Funnel-Stage Specific
- Biggest ROI opportunity: **View → Cart improvement**
- Secondary: **Cart recovery optimization**
- Tertiary: **Category scaling strategy**

👉 Interpretation: Small improvements at top-of-funnel = massive revenue impact.
