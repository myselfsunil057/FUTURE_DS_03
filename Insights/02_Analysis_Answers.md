## 🧪 Analysis Answers

This section provides data-backed answers derived from the e-commerce funnel dashboard analyzing 100M+ user interactions across Views → Carts → Orders.

---

## 🧭 Funnel Performance & Conversion

### What percentage of users move from product views → cart → purchase?
- Views → Carts: **3.8% (4M / 104M)**
- Carts → Orders: **~50% conversion (2M / 4M)**
- Overall Views → Orders: **~1.9%**

👉 Interpretation: Only 2 out of every 100 users complete a purchase. Extremely leaky top-of-funnel.

---

### Where is the largest drop-off point?
- Biggest drop: **Views → Carts (96.2% loss)**  
- Secondary drop: **Cart → Order (58% abandonment)**

👉 Insight: Awareness is not translating into intent. The primary failure is at **product engagement stage**, not checkout.

---

### How efficient is the platform in converting traffic into revenue?
- Conversion efficiency: **1.9% overall**
- Revenue per order: **$304 (high-quality AOV)**

👉 Insight: When users convert, value is strong — but acquisition-to-intent conversion is weak.

---

### How does conversion vary over time?
- Conversion spikes sharply during **Week 46**
- Baseline remains stable but low (~1.5–2%)

👉 Insight: Growth is **event-driven, not organic**.

---

## 👁️ User Behavior & Engagement

### How do users interact before purchasing?
- Majority of users only browse (104M views vs 4M carts)
- Very low engagement depth before cart action

👉 Insight: Users are not receiving enough purchase triggers (pricing clarity, trust signals, or product appeal).

---

### Which categories have high views but low engagement?
- Electronics: High traffic, moderate conversion
- Unknown category: High volume, low interpretability
- Stationery: High views, low cart conversion

👉 Insight: Visibility ≠ interest. Some categories attract curiosity but not intent.

---

### What indicates purchase intent vs casual browsing?
- High intent signals:
  - Cart addition (strongest signal)
  - Repeat category interactions
- Weak intent:
  - Single view sessions with no cart action (dominant behavior)

---

### How significant is attention loss?
- Attention Loss Rate: **96.2%**

👉 Insight: This is the **largest performance bottleneck in the entire system**.

---

## 🛒 Cart & Checkout Analysis

### Cart abandonment rate
- **58% of carts are abandoned**

👉 Insight: More than half of high-intent users drop before purchase.

---

### Which categories have highest abandonment?
- Electronics (high volume → high abandonment impact)
- Appliances
- Computers

👉 Insight: High-ticket categories increase hesitation and decision delay.

---

### Value of abandoned carts
- Cart Value: **$1.19B total**
- Lost revenue exposure is extremely high

👉 Insight: Even a **10% recovery = massive revenue uplift opportunity**

---

### Likely friction points
- Unexpected pricing/shipping costs
- Checkout complexity
- Lack of trust/payment flexibility
- No urgency-based nudges

---
