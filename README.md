# 📊 E-Commerce Intelligence Dashboard

## 🧠 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **4-page E-Commerce Intelligence Dashboard** built using Power BI, analyzing over **100M+ customer interaction records** from an online multi-category retail platform (Oct–Nov 2019).

The dashboard maps the **complete customer journey** — from product views → cart additions → final purchases — to uncover conversion bottlenecks, revenue drivers, and behavioral patterns.

The objective is to transform raw behavioral data into **actionable growth insights for funnel optimization and revenue improvement**.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze full e-commerce conversion funnel (Views → Carts → Orders)
- Identify critical drop-off points in customer journey
- Evaluate revenue, conversion rate, and engagement efficiency
- Detect category-wise performance patterns
- Support data-driven decisions for growth optimization

## 📊 Key Business Insights

### 🚨 Critical Insight — Attention Breakdown
A massive **96.2% view-to-cart drop-off** indicates severe engagement failure. Users are browsing but not convinced to take action, pointing to issues in product presentation, pricing clarity, or UX friction.

---

### ⚠️ High Risk — Cart Abandonment (58%)
More than half of high-intent users abandon checkout, signaling:
- Checkout friction
- Unexpected pricing/shipping costs
- Lack of trust or payment flexibility

---

### 🚀 Strong Signal — Revenue Growth (+120% MoM)
Revenue doubled in a single month, largely driven by a **Week 46 spike event**, indicating campaign-driven rather than organic growth.

---

### 📌 Behavioral Insight — Week 46 Spike
All funnel stages show synchronized surge in Week 46:
- Likely promotional campaign / festive sale
- Sharp temporary demand surge (~350K orders/week peak)
- Not sustained baseline growth

---

### 💰 Category Insight — Electronics Dominance
- Electronics drives majority of revenue and traffic
- High-ticket items inflate average order value
- Creates portfolio concentration risk

---

### 🔍 Hidden Opportunity — Kids Category
- Highest conversion rate (~50%)
- Underexposed in traffic allocation
- Strong candidate for scaling via targeted campaigns

---

### ⚠️ Data Quality Issue — “Unknown” Category
- Second-largest category in views and carts
- Indicates tagging/pipeline failure
- Distorts decision-making accuracy

---

## ⚠️ Business Risks Identified
- Extreme funnel leakage at early stage (attention failure)
- High cart abandonment reducing revenue efficiency
- Overdependence on Electronics category
- Data integrity issues in category classification

---

## 🚀 Growth Opportunities

- Optimize product page UX to reduce 96% attention loss
- Implement cart recovery systems (email, retargeting, incentives)
- Scale high-conversion categories (Kids, Accessories, Furniture)
- Replicate Week 46 campaign structure for repeatable growth spikes
- Fix data pipeline issues to improve category-level decision accuracy
